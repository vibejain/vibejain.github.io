AI Outreach Engine

A platform that prospects, writes tailored outreach, auto-generates a visual mockup of the proposed solution, and runs timely follow-ups.

  • LLM Research & Copy
  • RAG
  • AI Image Generation
  • Follow-ups
  • CRM

The Problem

Cold outreach fails when it is generic, and even good leads go cold when no one chases the follow-up. Describing a proposed solution in text also leaves the prospect guessing what the work would actually look like.

What I Built

A platform that runs outbound end to end. It finds and researches prospects, writes a genuinely tailored message for each one, and then does the part most tools skip: it auto-generates a visual mockup of the proposed solution and sends it along, so the client can actually see the work that could be done for them. Engagement is tracked, replies are routed to the CRM as qualified hand-offs, and non-responses move into a timely, cadence-based follow-up so nothing slips.

How It Works

1 · RESEARCHFind & research prospects from real data.
2 · WRITEWrite a genuinely tailored message for each.
3 · VISUALIZEAuto-generate a visual mockup of the solution.
4 · TRACKSend, track engagement, route replies to CRM.
5 · FOLLOW-UPCadence-based follow-ups so nothing slips.

Why It Matters

  • Personalized at scale: each message is built from real research, not a blast.
  • The generated mockup helps prospects visualize the solution, which lifts replies.
  • Timely, automated follow-ups mean promising conversations are never dropped.
  • Replaces a manual, inconsistent outbound process with a repeatable system.

Tech Stack

  • LLM research & copy (Claude / Llama)
  • RAG over prospect data
  • AI image generation (diffusion)
  • Email & deliverability
  • Follow-up cadence
  • CRM integration
  • Python / FastAPI
