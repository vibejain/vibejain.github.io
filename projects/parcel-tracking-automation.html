Parcel-Tracking & Delay-Notifier Automation

Tracks every delivery a customer has in flight, sends timely milestone updates, and proactively flags delays.

  • Automation
  • Carrier APIs
  • Email
  • Delay Alerts
  • Escalation

The Problem

Keeping customers updated on shipment status meant someone manually checking tracking and writing update emails. It didn't scale, updates were often late or missed, and worse — delays usually surfaced too late, often only when a customer complained.

What I Built

An automated email system that monitors the delivery status of everything a customer is working on and triggers the right communication automatically. On a routine milestone (picked up, in transit, out for delivery, delivered) it sends a timely, personalized update. When a shipment slips its ETA or hits an exception, it sends a proactive delay alert to the customer and escalates to the internal team so someone can act before it becomes a complaint. Status is tracked per shipment, so each event is communicated exactly once.

How It Works

1 · POLLPoll carrier APIs for every active shipment.
2 · COMPARECompare each shipment to its last known state.
3 · NOTIFYSend a personalized milestone update — exactly once.
4 · ESCALATEOn ETA slip/exception, alert customer + escalate internally.

Why It Matters

  • Customers stay informed at every milestone without manual effort.
  • Delays become early, actionable warnings instead of silent failures.
  • Internal teams are escalated in time to act, protecting the relationship.
  • Eliminates a recurring, low-value manual task and the cost of late surprises.

Tech Stack

  • Carrier APIs
  • Status state machine
  • ETA/SLA delay detection
  • LLM-worded emails
  • Email API
  • Escalation
